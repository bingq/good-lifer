# Privacy Policy for Good Lifer

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Introduction

Good Lifer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Storage Only

Good Lifer is designed with privacy as a core principle. **All data is stored locally on your device** using the device's local storage system (AsyncStorage). This means:

- Your achievements, journal entries, and categories are stored only on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- No data is collected by us or any analytics services

### Types of Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device:

1. **Achievements**: Your logged achievements including category, name, quantity, unit, notes, and timestamps
2. **Tree Hole Entries**: Your private journal entries and their timestamps
3. **Categories**: Your achievement categories and their configurations
4. **User Profile**: Any profile information you choose to provide (future feature)

### Data Access

- Only you can access your data through the App
- Your data is not accessible to us, the developers, or any third parties
- Your data is not backed up to cloud services unless you explicitly choose to export it

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to anyone.** Your data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

Good Lifer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

## Permissions

Good Lifer requests the following permissions (if applicable):

- **Microphone**: Not currently used. This permission may be requested by Expo framework but is not utilized by Good Lifer.
- **Camera**: Not used. Good Lifer does not access your camera.
- **Photo Library**: Not used. Good Lifer does not access your photo library.

## Data Export

You can export your data at any time through the Settings screen. The export feature prepares your data in JSON format. You are responsible for how you store or share exported data.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the "Clear All Data" option in Settings. This action permanently deletes all achievements, journal entries, and categories from your device. This action cannot be undone.

## Children's Privacy

Good Lifer is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any information, this is not a concern.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- You have complete control over your data
- You can delete your data at any time
- You can export your data at any time
- You can uninstall the App, which will remove all local data (unless you've exported it)
