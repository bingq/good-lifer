# Privacy Policy for Good Lifer

**Last Updated: February 2, 2026**

## Introduction

Good Lifer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

Good Lifer is designed to be completely private and offline. All your data is stored locally on your device and never leaves your device.

## Information Storage

All information you enter into Good Lifer, including:
- Daily achievements
- Projects and goals
- Tree Hole entries
- Categories
- Settings and preferences

...is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage).

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Usage data or analytics
- Device information
- Location data
- Crash reports
- Any other data

## Third-Party Services

Good Lifer does not use any third-party services, including:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- Authentication services

## Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, we don't share any data with third parties.

## Data Export

You can export your data at any time through Settings → Data Management → Export Data. This creates a JSON file that you can save anywhere you choose.

## Children's Privacy

Good Lifer does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
